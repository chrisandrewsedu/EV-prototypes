
### **Epic Feature: Fallacy Finders**

#### **Summary:**

Fallacy Finders is an interactive, educational game hosted on the Empowered.Vote platform, designed to enhance critical thinking and civic engagement. It combines elements of bar trivia and multiplayer video gaming, with a focus on identifying logical fallacies in civic content. Key aspects include:

* **Team-Based Gameplay**: Players join or create a lobby, forming teams to play rounds of the game, each lasting about 10-15 minutes.  
* **Random Fallacy Assignment**: At the start of each round, players are randomly assigned a logical fallacy to spot in the provided content.  
* **Content Analysis**: Teams watch pre-recorded civic content, such as debates or newscasts, and identify logical fallacies in real-time.  
* **Point System and Competition**: Points are awarded for correctly identifying fallacies, with a scoring system that rewards quick and accurate identification.  
* **Educational Integration**: The game educates players about logical fallacies, potentially integrating with Empowered Badges for deeper learning.

#### **User Stories**:

* **Joining and Playing the Game**:  
  * As a user, I want to easily join or create a game of Fallacy Finders with friends or like-minded individuals.  
  * Acceptance Criteria:  
    * Users can join or create a lobby via the Empowered.Vote platform.  
    * The game facilitates team formation and matchmaking.  
        
* **Identifying Logical Fallacies**:  
  * As a player, I need to identify and flag logical fallacies assigned to me in civic content.  
  * Acceptance Criteria:  
    * Players are assigned logical fallacies randomly at each round's start.  
    * A mechanism (such as pressing a spacebar) allows players to flag fallacies.  
        
* **Engaging and Educational Gameplay**:  
  * As a player, I want the game to be both engaging and educational, enhancing my understanding of logical fallacies.  
  * Acceptance Criteria:  
    * The game provides an enjoyable and competitive experience.  
    * Players have access to educational resources about logical fallacies.

#### **Potential Tasks:**

* **Game Lobby and Matchmaking Development**:  
  * Create a system for players to join/create game lobbies.  
  * Implement a matchmaking algorithm for forming teams.  
      
* **Fallacy Assignment and Content Integration**:  
  * Develop a randomizer for logical fallacy assignments.  
  * Curate and integrate diverse civic content for gameplay.  
      
* **Gameplay Mechanics and Scoring System**:  
  * Design and implement gameplay mechanics, including the fallacy flagging system.  
  * Create a scoring system based on speed and accuracy of fallacy identification.  
      
* **Educational Content and Badge Integration**:  
  * Develop educational materials about each logical fallacy.  
  * Integrate with the Empowered Badges system for extended learning opportunities.  
      
* **Platform Technical Infrastructure**:  
  * Build and maintain a robust technical infrastructure to support real-time gameplay and content streaming.  
  * Ensure data privacy and security, especially for connected accounts.  
      
* **User Feedback and Accessibility**:  
  * Establish channels for user feedback and continuously iterate based on this input.  
  * Prioritize accessibility and inclusive design in all aspects of the game.  
    

Fallacy Finders aims to provide an entertaining yet educational platform for players to enhance their critical thinking skills, particularly in analyzing civic content, while fostering a community of informed and engaged citizens.
